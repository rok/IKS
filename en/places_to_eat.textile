h2. Places to eat

!../slike/Prehrana.jpg!

<b>(1) Minutka</b>
Nice restaurant with pizza, pasta, excellent food and daily menu.

<b>(2) Čuk</b>
Restaurant in the Sport park with plenty of space. Although known as pizzeria, they also serve good pasta, traditional local and "global" food, incl. salad bar and daily menu.

<b>(3) Bar Bor</b>
Traditional local daily meals. Good and cheap.  

<b>(4) Šport hotel</b>
Also serves daily meals.

<b>(5) Špajza</b>
Fot those looking for fine ambient and food for moderate price.

<b>(5) Proteus</b>
Renewed restaurant with all sorts of food. In the main square below the Instutute.
